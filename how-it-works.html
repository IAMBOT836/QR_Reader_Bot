How It Works

A simple six-step process that transforms traditional product manuals into accessible digital experiences.

1

Create Manual

Enter product instructions and information into the system.

↓
2

Generate QR Code

Convert product information into a downloadable QR code.

↓
3

Attach To Product

Place the generated QR code on the product packaging.

↓
4

User Scans QR

Customers scan the QR code using their smartphone camera.

↓
5

Manual Opens

Product instructions become available instantly on the device.

↓
6

Listen or Read

Users can either read the manual or listen using Text-to-Speech.
