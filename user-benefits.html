User Benefits

QR-Based User Manual Bot provides advantages for users, manufacturers, and society by making product information more accessible and efficient.

♿ Accessibility

Supports visually impaired and elderly users through Text-to-Speech technology.

⚡ Saves Time

Instantly access instructions without searching through lengthy manuals.

🌱 Eco-Friendly

Reduces paper usage and promotes sustainable product documentation.

📱 Mobile Access

Access manuals anywhere using a smartphone or tablet.

🌍 Global Reach

Future multilingual support allows worldwide accessibility.

🔊 Audio Instructions

Listen to instructions instead of reading complicated documents.

Who Benefits?

👵 Elderly Users

Large digital text and audio support improve usability and understanding.

👨‍🎓 Students

Quick access to instructions and educational content through QR codes.

👨‍🦯 Visually Impaired Users

Audio-based guidance removes the need to read printed manuals.

🏭 Manufacturers
