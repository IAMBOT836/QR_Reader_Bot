About QR-Based User Manual Bot

QR-Based User Manual Bot is an innovative solution designed to transform traditional paper manuals into interactive digital experiences. By combining QR code technology with text-to-speech functionality, users can instantly access product instructions through their smartphones.

🎯 Our Mission

To make product manuals accessible, user-friendly, and available to everyone regardless of age, literacy level, or physical limitations.

🌎 Our Vision

To eliminate dependence on paper manuals and create a future where every product offers digital, accessible, and intelligent support.

📖 Why We Built This

Many users struggle with traditional manuals because they are lengthy, difficult to understand, easy to lose, and often printed in small fonts. Elderly individuals and visually impaired users face even greater challenges.

QR-Based User Manual Bot solves this problem by allowing users to scan a QR code and instantly access product instructions. The instructions can also be converted into speech, making them easier to understand and use.
