How It Works

  1. Step 1: Scan the QR Code on the product using your device.
  2. Step 2: The bot fetches the digital product manual from its database.
  3. Step 3: The manual is converted into audio instructions using text-to-speech technology.
  4. Step 4: Listen to the step-by-step instructions for easy product setup and use.

This process ensures that users can easily understand and operate products without the need for physical manuals.
