About Us

We created the QR-Based User Manual Bot to revolutionize how people access product manuals. Our goal is to make product usage easier, especially for those who face challenges with traditional manuals. By combining QR code technology and text-to-speech conversion, we bring a seamless experience for all users.

Our mission is to enhance accessibility, reduce paper waste, and provide a smarter way to interact with products. Join us on our journey to simplify the future!
